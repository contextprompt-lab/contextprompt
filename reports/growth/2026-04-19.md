## Competitor Research Summary

**Read.ai** (read.ai):
- Pricing: Free (5 meetings/month), Pro $19.75/user/mo (annual), Enterprise $29.75/user/mo
- Differentiator: Meeting engagement scores, talk-time analytics, video highlights, Salesforce/Notion sync
- Weakness: No repo awareness, no code-specific task extraction, per-seat pricing expensive for small teams
- contextprompt wins on: repo-aware output, per-account pricing ($30/mo vs ~$99/mo for 5-person team)

**Fellow** (fellow.ai):
- Pricing: Free plan, Pro $7/user/mo, Business $15/user/mo, Enterprise $25/user/mo
- Differentiator: Collaborative agendas, 1-on-1 frameworks, "Ask Fellow" meeting search, native Jira/Linear sync
- Weakness: No code context in tasks, no file path extraction, no AI coding agent output
- contextprompt wins on: repo-aware task extraction, implementation steps, AI coding assistant output
