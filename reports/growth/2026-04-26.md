## Competitor Research Summary

**Avoma** (avoma.com):
- Pricing: ~$19/user/mo annual; no permanent free tier (14-day trial only); per-seat model
- Differentiator: AI scorecards, deal risk tracking, real-time Answer Cards for sales reps, two-way CRM sync (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive)
- Target: sales and revenue teams, SMBs
- Weakness: sales-centric — no codebase awareness, no coding tasks, limited appeal outside sales; transcription accuracy issues reported
- contextprompt wins on: repo-aware task extraction, per-account pricing ($30/mo vs ~$95/mo for 5-person team), permanent free tier, developer focus

**Krisp** (krisp.ai):
- Pricing: Free (60 min/day noise cancel, 2 summaries), Pro $16/user/mo, Business $30/user/mo
- Differentiator: industry-leading noise cancellation at OS audio level, works with any conferencing app, 16-language transcription, AI Chat about meetings, MCP server (beta)
- Target: general knowledge workers, remote teams prioritizing audio quality
- Weakness: basic/shallow AI notes (not developer-specific), no codebase awareness, no coding task output, inconsistent transcription accuracy
- contextprompt wins on: repo-aware task extraction, AI coding assistant output, per-account pricing for teams of 2+, no install required
