### PR #13 — Fix meta descriptions + add FAQ schema to use-case pages
**Branch:** `agent/seo/fix-use-case-ctrs`

**Root cause fixed:** All 4 use-case pages had meta descriptions over 160 chars (201–220 chars). Google truncates descriptions at ~160 chars, showing "..." in search results — this was destroying CTR on pages already ranking in positions 4-8.

Also added 5-question FAQ sections + FAQPage JSON-LD schema to the two highest-traffic pages to target People Also Ask SERP features.
